Fruits Definition List

Apple
A sweet, typically red or green fruit, often eaten raw or used in cooking.
Banana
A long, curved fruit with a yellow peel and soft, sweet flesh.
Orange
A round citrus fruit with a tough skin and juicy, tangy flesh.
Grapes
Small, round, typically purple or green fruits, often eaten raw or used to make wine.
Pineapple
A tropical fruit with a spiky skin and sweet, tangy flesh.
